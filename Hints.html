Hints

How to make video tracks

1: Press play for playing the video.
2: Slide for start time.
3: Press pause.
4: Press start time
5: Press play again.
6: Slide for end time.
7: Press pause.
8: Press end time.
9: Insert filename, trackname and press save button.

Automatically saves the play sequence

1: Click or touch on the record play sequence button.
2: Animate your scene.
3: Click or touch on the stop record play sequence when done.

Screen recording
×
1: Click on "App".
2: Click on "Setup recording".
3: Choose "Entire screen,window or Chrome Tab".
4: Choose to share audio.
5: Click Share.
6: Next you will see the message "Start recording".
7: Click on "Start". to start recording.

8: Click "pause" to pause the recording and click start to resume the recording.

9: Click "Stop" to save the recording.
